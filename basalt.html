Basalt
Basalt is a very common dark-colored volcanic rock
composed of calcic plagioclase (usually labradorite),
clinopyroxene (augite) and iron ore (titaniferous magnetite).
Basalt may also contain olivine, quartz,
hornblende, nepheline, orthopyroxene, etc.
Basalt is a volcanic equivalent of gabbro.
Oceanic crust is mostly composed of basalt
or rocks with the same composition (gabbro, diabase).
