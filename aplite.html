Aplite
Aplite is a fine-grained and usually light-colored plutonic rock.
Aplite usually solidified in a narrow crack where heat loss
to the adjacent rocks was rapid and crystals
did not have much time to grow in size.
Aplites may have a variable composition from granite to gabbro,
but the term “aplite” is mostly used to describe fine-grained granitic rocks.
